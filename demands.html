The Fight the Fees campaign demands that the goverment commit to the following:

No Fees

Students are calling for the progressive reduction and elimination of tuition fees for ALL students, centering First Nations, MÃ©tis and Inuit learners who have been denied their treaty rights to education and includes international students who have been unfairly left out of the tuition fee framework.

No Loans

Students are calling for the conversion of student loans into non-repayable grants in the 2017 Ontario Budget, and for an end to the unfair practice of excluding part-time students in the Ontario Student Grant.

No Debt

Students are calling for the removal of interest on existing student loans, and the immediate introduction of debt relief programs geared towards low and middle income students and alumni.
